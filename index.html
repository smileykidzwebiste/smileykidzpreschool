A Day Filled with Learning & Smiles

Every day is a new adventure! Here's what a typical day looks like for our little ones.

9:00 AM

Morning Welcome & Energizing Start

The day begins with prayer and light exercises to help children feel fresh, focused, and ready to learn.

9:30 AM

Learning Time

Structured classroom sessions where children build strong basics through interactive and engaging teaching methods.

11:00AM

Snacks & Break Time

Healthy snack time to refresh and recharge. Playgroup & Nursery children have snacks at 11:00 AM, while PP1, PP2 & Grade 1 students have their break at 12:00 PM.

12:30 PM

Activities & Continued Learning

A mix of fun activities, creative play, and guided learning sessions that support overall development and joyful engagement.
